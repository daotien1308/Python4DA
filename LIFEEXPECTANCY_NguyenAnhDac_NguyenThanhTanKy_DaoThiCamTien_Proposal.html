Ý nghĩa của các biến có trong dataset:¶

  • Country: t nc
  • Year: nm
  • Status: tnh trng ang pht trin hay pht trin
  • Life expectancy: K vng tui th (age)
  • Adult Mortality: T l t vong ngi trng thnh15-60 tui ( trn 1000 dn s))
  • infant deaths: S tr s sinh t vong ( trn 1000 dn s)
  • Alcohol: ru tiu th bnh qun u ngi (>15 age) (tnh bng lt ru nguyn cht)
  • percentage expenditure: Chi cho y t tnh theo phn trm Tng sn phm quc ni trn u ngi (%)
  • Hepatitis B: T l tim chng Vim gan B (HepB) tr 1 tui (%)
  • Measles: bnh s - s ca c bo co (trn 1000 dn s)
  • BMI: Ch s khi c th
  • under-five deaths: S ngi cht di 5 tui (trn 1000 dn))
  • Polio: Bi lit tr 1 tui (%)
  • Total expenditure: Chi tiu chung ca chnh ph cho y t tnh theo t l phn trm ca tng chi tiu ca chnh ph (%)
  • Diphtheria: T l tim chng gii c t un vn v ho g (DTP3) cho tr 1 tui (%)
  • HIV/AIDS: T vong trn 1000 tr sinh sng HIV / AIDS (0-4 tui)
  • GDP: Tng sn phm quc ni bnh qun u ngi (tnh bng USD)
  • Population: Dn s
  • thinness 1-19 years:T l gy tr em v thanh thiu nin t 10 n 19 tui (%)
  • thinness 5-9 years: T l gy tr em t 5-9 tui (%)
  • Income composition of resources:Ch s pht trin con ngi v c cu thu nhp ca ti nguyn (ch s t 0 n 1)
  • Schooling: S nm i hc (nm)
